TAO

TAO is my simple but not easy project with a grand vision transplanted from dreams into reality on the off chance that it may succeed, even if in 50 years or more.

It is meant to be a truly universal and modular syntax for ultra-large scale structural communication.

I imagine future evolutions of formats like JSON, HTML, XML, S-expressions that all share TAO as their underlying syntax.

Future aside, I am sharing it because I simply would love if something like it would be widely available and supported. Above all it is great fun for me to just develop a pet project like this.

I hope you have as much fun using it as I have in developing it.

See https://tree-annotation.org/ (mirror here, not necessarily up-to-date), https://github.com/tree-annotation or contact me at dariusz.jedrzejczak.work for more details.
